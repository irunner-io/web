How it works

GitHub orchestrates. You execute.

Your workflow defines what it needs via labels. iRunner matches labels to pools and allocates compute — warm or cold — inside your private network.

1

Job queued

GitHub sends a webhook to your control plane.

2

Label matched

runs-on label maps to a pool with defined specs.

3

Resource allocated

Warm pool claimed instantly, or cold-provisioned.

4

Ephemeral execution

Runs in private subnet. Destroyed after completion.

Compliance & Security
